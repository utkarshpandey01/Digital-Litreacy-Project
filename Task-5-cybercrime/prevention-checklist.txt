Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone.
2. Do not click on unknown links received through SMS or email.
3. Always verify the phone number of customer care before calling.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
5. Enable two-factor authentication where possible.
6. Do not use public Wi-Fi for banking or online payments.
7. Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history.
8. If you become a victim of cybercrime, report it immediately on cybercrime.gov.in or call helpline number 1930.
